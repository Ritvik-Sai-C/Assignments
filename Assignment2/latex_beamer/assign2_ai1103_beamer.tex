	\section{Question}
	\begin{frame}{Question 19.c}
	\

The average cost function associated with producing and marketing x units of an item is given by AC $ = 2x - 11 + \frac{50}{x}$. Find the range of values of the output x, for which AC is increasing.\\

\end{frame}

% The solution
	\section{Solution}
	\begin{frame}{Solution}
	
	The average cost function associated with producing and marketing x units of an item is
 given as:\\
 AC $ =2x - 11 + \frac{50}{x}$\\
Output for which AC increases is: $\frac{d(AC)}{d(x)} > 0$ \\
\implies $$ \frac{d}{dx} (2x - 11 + \frac{50}{x}) > 0 $$ \\ 
\implies $ (x^2 - 25) > 0$ \\
\implies (x-5)(x+5) > 0 \\
\implies $$x > 5$ as $(x > 0)$$ \\
\therefore  $ The average cost increases  when output $x > 5.$$ \\

\end{frame}

% The answer
	\section{Answer}
	\begin{frame}{Answer}
	In the range of $ x \in (5,\infty)$ , the average cost function increases.
	
\end{frame}
